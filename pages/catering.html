GROUP OF RESTAURANTS

Food Service by Carrier

We provide food service by carrier method for our customers whose number of personnel is between 20 and 300 from companies, which operating in Baku. Meals are produced in our own kitchens in accordance with quality standards and delivered to the customer's address, waiter and cleaning staff are appointed upon request, and pre-dinner preparation and post-dinner cleaning and dining hall arrangements are made.

On-site Meal Production

For companies with more than 80 people and a kitchen area, our catering factory produces meals in the customer's kitchen, and then provides on-site service and cleaning services.
